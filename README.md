### 🗺️ HunterMania — Location-based Treasure Hunt App
> A Flutter app where players explore real-world gardens, solve GPS-gated clues, and race the clock across beautifully designed hunts.

**Stack:** Flutter · Firebase · BLoC · Google Maps · RevenueCat · Clean Architecture

- 🔐 Google Sign-In & Firebase Auth
- 📍 Haversine-based GPS geofencing for checkpoint unlock
- 💎 RevenueCat-powered paywall & subscriptions
- 🧩 Two checkpoint types: clue answers & photo tasks
- 📊 Real-time Firestore progress tracking
- 🌙 Dark-first glassmorphic UI with Poppins typography
